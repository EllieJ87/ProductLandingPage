About

Welcome to my initial product page! I'm Ellie and I hand knit and crochet one off pieces
I can cutom make anything you wish so if you have a style or a pattern in mind and not a good knitter I'm here to help you!
I hand knit and crochet patterns that either I create and can design and adapt myself. I also reasearch a lot of new patterns and can tailor these to any needs to what I feel works best!
The materials I mainly use are cottons (mainly for the Baby wear as this is easier to care for) and natural and organic fibres.
I'm looking into more sustainable and recycled sources to help with the environment. I use respectful resources to get these chosen materials from.

Please contact me your requirements or if you have any questions!

How it Works

Pick a clothing type - I can knit Adult, Baby clothing and soft furnishes

Pick a design style - If you have a pattern, style or a stitch in mind feel free to submit an image and if you have a vague pattern the better!

Pick a Material - If you have a material in mind or can't wear a particular one please let me know and I can give you options on what I feel works best!

Pick a Colour - If you have a colour in mind, wear certain colours or just want to try a new colour feel free to let me know this!
